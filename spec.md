Product Specification Document: Qanoun Tech (قانون تك)1. Product OverviewName: Qanoun Tech (قانون تك)Description: An Arabic-language Legal Technology SaaS platform tailored for the Egyptian and broader Arab market. It connects clients with legal professionals while providing practice management tools for lawyers and law firms.2. Target AudienceClients: Individuals or entities seeking legal consultations, representation, or document review.Lawyers: Independent legal practitioners needing case management, booking systems, and a platform to offer consultations.Law Offices: Legal firms requiring multi-user collaboration, firm-wide case management, and aggregate billing.3. Technology StackFrontend: Next.js 16 (App Router), TypeScript 5.UI & Styling: Tailwind CSS v4, shadcn/ui, Recharts, next-themes (forced dark mode), Lucide React.Typography: Cairo (Google Fonts — Arabic/Latin).Backend & Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL).Authentication: Auth.js (NextAuth v5) with Supabase Adapter (handling JWT session roles).Testing: TestSprite (Full-stack automated testing via MCP).Deployment: Vercel.4. User Roles & AccessRoleScopeKey CapabilitiesClientPersonalSearch for lawyers, book appointments, submit legal questions, track personal case status.LawyerProfessionalManage individual cases and tasks, answer consultations, update public profile, view personal analytics.OfficeAdministrativeManage firm profile, assign cases to team members, view aggregate analytics and billing, oversee firm-wide consultations.5. Core FeaturesDiscovery & Search (/discovery): Directory filtering to match clients with the right lawyers or offices.Role-Based Dashboards (/dashboard/[role]): Segregated, secure workspaces with custom sidebars depending on the authenticated role.Consultation System (/consultations): Workflow for clients to submit questions (/ask) and professionals to provide legal answers.Booking Engine (/booking): Scheduling interface for clients to book time with specific lawyers or offices.Analytics Dashboard: Visual data representation (using Recharts) for tracking case progress, task completion, and financial metrics.6. Technical & Non-Functional RequirementsLocalization: Strictly Arabic-first. UI must enforce lang="ar" and dir="rtl" at the root level.Authentication Flow: Secure, server-side session management. localStorage must not be used for access control. Unauthenticated traffic must route to /login.Type Safety: Strict TypeScript adherence required for all new components and database schemas.Responsive Design: Fully mobile-optimized across all dashboard and public-facing routes.
